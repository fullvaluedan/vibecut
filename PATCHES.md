## Chunked export audio mix (W1, 2026-07-20)

Removes the long-video "createBuffer wall": the export audio stage used to allocate ONE `AudioBuffer` for the whole timeline, which the browser refuses past ~21 min stereo @ 44.1kHz (~459 MB), so a 21 min+ export hard-failed. New pure module `media/export-chunk-mixer.ts` (ours) + additions to `media/timeline-audio-size.ts` (ours) do the windowed mixing; the three upstream files below are the wiring.
